“VASUDHAIVA KUTUMBAKAM” is a Sanskrit phrase that translates to “THE WORLD IS ONE FAMILY”

It’s an ancient Indian concept that promotes the idea of the entire world as a single interconnected family, where people from all walks of life and backgrounds are considered part of the same global.

This principle emphasizes unity, peace, and the understanding that all humans share a common bond.

In INDIA, we believe in “ATITHI DEVO BHAVA” which means “THE GUEST IS GOD”.

So you have to come and visit INDIA to experience this and make INDIA your “SECOND HOME”.
